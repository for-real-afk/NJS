About Us

At NJS Pharmaceutical, we provide world-class standard quality products, also we are dedicated to the dynamic realm of drug discovery, and development, providing the community with the best products and services. Our commitment to innovation drives progress in healthcare all over. and we are focusing on the betterment of physical, mental, and social well-being.

NJS Pharma, established in July 2013, is a dynamic and rapidly growing pharmaceutical company dedicated to enhancing the healthcare landscape across India. With a strong presence in 5 states, we specialize in the distribution of high-quality cardiac and diabetes products. Our extensive reach is supported by our 3 strategically located CFAs in Nort-East India, West Bengal and Ahmedabad, facilitating efficient and timely deliveries. NJS operates from two key offices: our Registration Office and Corporate Office in Mumbai. We are driven by a commitment to providing accessible, reliable, and innovative pharmaceutical solutions to improve the well-being of people throughout the nation.
